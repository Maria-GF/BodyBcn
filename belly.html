Bellydance

Oriental Dance, Raqs Sharki, Belly dance or also called Belly Dance is the scenic evolution influenced by ballet and other dances and currents of various dances from the Middle East.

Oriental dance is characterized by movements of the hips and torso, soft and undulating but also strong and dry. In the classes we work on postural correction to seek the elegance of our movements and avoid injuries, as well as technique and interpretation.

Folklore is also worked, and elements such as veils, fans, saber, etc ...
