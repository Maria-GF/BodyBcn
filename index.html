Dance & Pilates Studio

About us

Body BCN is a space in which we offer you different disciplines to be able to work your body in a healthy and healthy way.

What we offer

We offer classes of different types of dance to disciplines such as Yoga or Pilates so that you can choose the one that best suits your way of being and personal options.

We adapt to you

Our classes are in the morning and afternoon so you can choose the moment in which you want to dedicate that time so necessary for your body.

Our services

We offer different dance styles at varying levels and include many preferences and experience levels

Our teachers

BodyBCN is made up of a team of experienced teaching professionals who love their jobs. In any of the activities proposed by BODYBCN, the teachers will accompany you on your way of learning, overcoming and improving on a technical and artistic level.

Aysel

Bellydance

Aysel is the academy director and teacher of Pilates, Oriental Dance, Gypsy, Burlesque, and Poi Dance.She has trained with dancers such as Norma Ros, Lesya Starr, Lena Nouar, Óscar Flores, Rachida Arrahat, Álika ... and currently belongs to the "Óscar Flores Troupe". Since 2009 she has been working as a teacher and dancer.

Alexis Sulbarán

Contemporary

Graduate in Contemporary Dance from Unearte. He studied with the teacher Rumen Rachev in the Ballet of the Americas. Obtains a scholarship to the National Ballet School of Cuba. He has worked with recognized companies: Dramo, SieteOcho, Free Agent, El Eco de Los Ciruelos National Theater Company.

Marta Eres

Tribal

Marta is a performer in her beginnings, she studied rhythmic gymnastics, classical oriental dances and folklores until she reached ATS and tribal fusion. She also traveled through other dances such as flamenco, hip hop or ballet, dances that inspire her and help shape her style. Creator of Integral Dance for Tribal Fusión®, 2013

Marta Sánchez

Pilates

His sports training also includes fitness (aerobics, stretching, toning ...)
