Pilates

Pilates is a physical and mental training system created at the beginning of the 20th century by Joseph Hubertus Pilates, who devised it based on his knowledge of different specialties such as gymnastics, traumatology and yoga; combining dynamism and muscular strength with mental control, breathing and relaxation.

Pre/Post Natal Pilates

The practice of Pilates both during pregnancy and after childbirth is highly recommended, since the exercise can be adapted to each moment of pregnancy according to the needs, and in the postpartum period it is especially indicated for the recovery of both the pelvic floor and the transverse floor.
